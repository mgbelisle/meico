HANNAH UECKERT began her training in The Woodlands, TX at Excelsior School of Dance. She enjoyed ten years of training with Excelsior as well as traveling to China with the company. Studying under Jim and Bridget Payne, she performed in The Nutcracker, Coppelia, and Swan Lake. Upon graduating from High School, Hannah joined Ad Deum Dance Company as a trainee training with Randall Flinn in Houston, TX. She danced in Houston, Spain, and North Africa with Ad Deum II. She taught ballet for four years along side Jim and Bridget Payne and is now employed with Kelly Ann Vitacca here in The Woodlands, TX. As well as teaching, she is dancing with Open Sky Arts Collective, also here locally in The Woodlands, TX.
