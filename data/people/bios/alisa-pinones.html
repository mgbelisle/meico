Alisa Pinones is a graduate of Belhaven College with a Bachelor's of Art in Dance. In 2010 she traveled and performed in many places including South Aftrica, the Netherlands, and England. She moved back to her hometown after her travels and performed with modern dance company, Psophania. After marrying her husband God called Alisa to College Station. They are now proud parents of a 2 year old girl and 1 year old boy. Ms. Alisa is passionate about dance and worship, and she is honored to be a part of Mei/Co.
