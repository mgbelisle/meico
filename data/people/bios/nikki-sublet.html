Nicole (Nikki) Sublet grew up in the competitive dance world in the states of Wisconsin, Ohio, and Illinois. She attended Wright State University in Dayton, Ohio, where she graduated summa cum laude with a BFA in Dance in 2013. During her college training, Nikki was also a 3-year member of the Dayton Contemporary Dance Second Company. In 2014, she moved to Houston to join the Ad Deum Dance Second Company, and in 2016 she transitioned to Ad Deum's main company. After two and a half seasons with the main company, Nikki has completed her time with Ad Deum and will begin teaching dance full time for the arts program at Episcopal High School this fall.
