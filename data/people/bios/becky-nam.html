Becky Nam is a native of College Station, Texas, where she began dancing at age six at Suzanne's School of Dance. She is currently earning a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Dance at the University of Texas at Austin, where she has worked with choreographers such as Charles O. Anderson, Jenn Freeman, Lorenzo “Rennie” Harris, Yacov Sharir, and Gesel Mason. In 2016, she studied abroad at the Centre National de Danse Contemporaine under the direction of Robert Swinston in Angers, France. This past March, her solo work Rooting: Mother’s Stories was chosen for the regional Gala at the American College Dance Association conference. She most recently performed at Fusebox Festival in Charles O. Anderson’s evening-length work, (Re)current Unrest.
