Hannah Welch began her dance training in North Carolina. She attended the University of North Carolina School of the Arts and holds a BFA in dance performance. While at UNCSA, she had the privilege to perform works by Marius Petipa, Jerome Robbins, Agnes DeMille, Jose Limon, and Twyla Tharp along with original works by Susan McCullough, Brenda Daniels, Helen Simoneau, and Larry Keigwin. She has studied across the country with American Ballet Theatre, Pittsburgh Ballet Theatre, North Carolina Dance Theatre, Complexions, LINES, and Keigwin + CO. Hannah is currently dancing with Thomas Ortiz Dance. She is grateful to be a part of Awakening Movement.
