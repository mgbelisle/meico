The University Engagement Program is a means of reaching college students who may be interested in joining a Mei/Co. Dance project. We hope to engage in residencies with different Universities to share our work and our process of listening and creating with students. The art of listening is not a common practice these days and we want to bring that back to the forefront of education. We would love to come to your university for a masterclass or to set work.

Contact us at people@meico.dance to schedule.
