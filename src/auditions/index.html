Audition Tour Spring 2022

We are excited to do our first official audition tour throughout this Spring. Check back for more information and keep an eye on our social media for updates.

February 4th: Texas A&M University

We will be holding two separate auditions on this day at Texas A&M University in College Station.

Check in: 10:30am

Audition #1: 10:40am-12:00pm (Contemporary Ballet)

Audition #2: 12:20pm-1:40pm (Contemporary)

Anyone auditioning is encouraged to attend both audition slots. However, one is sufficient. Please specify which audition you will be attending on the application form below by 1/31.

February 18th: Belhaven University

Check in: 12:45pm

Audition: 1:00pm-2:30pm

We are always interested in meeting people who share our vision! We are a project based company currently working in the College Station, TX area. If you are interested in being involved in a specific project, or want to become a member of the Mei/Co. team, please fill out our application form.

Please also email a headshot, resume, and if possible, a dance reel to people@meico.dance

We look forward to dancing with you soon!
