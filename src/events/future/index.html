    A Stroll down Greenleaf Ln. with Mei/Co. Dance

    July 19th at 6:30pm

    Are you ready to get out of the house? Come join us for an evening of dancing and strolling down the street with Mei/Co. Dance. This evening will be a site specific dance performance in various locations around Greenleaf Ln. The audience will be lead down the lane to watch each performance until finally reaching a treehouse in the woods. Each dance speaks to the strength, courage, and hope of those who have battled cancer and hopes to leave the audience with a new vantage point of hope and vision for their lives.

    The performance will end with a light reception outside with snacks, drinks, and much needed face to face conversations.

    We hope to honor those who have walked through cancer by this presentation through the gift of dance.

    For more information see the Story Lane Program and email people@meico.dance
